<p><ul><li>Manual HVAC Systems</li><li>Automatic HVAC Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive HVAC technology market is primarily divided into two types: manual HVAC systems and automatic HVAC systems. Manual HVAC systems require users to manually set temperature and airflow settings, providing a basic level of climate control. In contrast, automatic HVAC systems use sensors and advanced algorithms to automatically adjust temperature and airflow based on real-time conditions, enhancing comfort and efficiency. The growing preference for user-friendly and energy-efficient solutions is driving the shift towards automatic systems in modern vehicles.</p></p>
