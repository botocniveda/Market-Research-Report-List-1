Horizontal waste heat boilers are typically more compact and suitable for industries with limited vertical space, allowing efficient heat recovery from exhaust gases. In contrast, vertical waste heat boilers occupy less floor area and are often preferred in scenarios where height is available, offering high efficiency in thermal recovery. Both types serve crucial roles in energy conservation and emissions reduction.</p></p>
