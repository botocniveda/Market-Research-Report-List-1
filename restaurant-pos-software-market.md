<p><p>The restaurant POS software market encompasses three primary types: cloud-based, on-premise, and hybrid systems. Cloud-based POS solutions offer flexibility and remote access, enabling real-time data management and updates through the internet. On-premise systems require local installation, providing full control over hardware and data security but often necessitate higher upfront costs. Hybrid systems combine both cloud and on-premise features, allowing restaurants to enjoy the benefits of remote access while maintaining local data storage and control.</p></p>
