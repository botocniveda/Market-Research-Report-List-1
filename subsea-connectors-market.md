<p><ul><li>Wet Pluggable Connector</li><li>Dry Mateable Submersible Connector</li><li>Wet Mateable-Pressure Balanced Oil Filled (PBOF) Connector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The subsea connectors market includes various types designed for underwater applications. Wet pluggable connectors allow for easy connection and disconnection while submerged, facilitating maintenance without surface retrieval. Dry mateable submersible connectors are designed for connections made in dry environments, suitable for subsea deployment after a surface assembly. Wet mateable-pressure balanced oil filled (PBOF) connectors maintain pressure balance, enabling reliable connections at great depths, ideal for high-performance subsea systems in oil and gas exploration and other marine industries.</p></p>
