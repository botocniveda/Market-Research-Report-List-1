<p><p>The single-use pH sensors market is categorized into digital and analog sensors. Digital pH sensors utilize advanced microelectronic technology to provide precise, real-time measurements, often integrating with digital devices for enhanced data analysis. In contrast, analog pH sensors offer a simpler design, functioning based on electrical potential differences without the need for digital processing. Both types serve various applications in pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and environmental monitoring, with digital sensors generally preferred for their accuracy and ease of integration into automated systems.</p></p>
