<p><p>Robotic Process Automation (RPA) tools are categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based RPA facilitates scalable automation solutions hosted on remote servers, allowing organizations to access tools via the internet. This model offers flexibility and lower upfront costs. Conversely, on-premises RPA requires organizations to install and maintain software on their own servers, providing greater control and security but often at a higher initial investment and maintenance cost. Both types address diverse organizational needs in automation.</p></p>
