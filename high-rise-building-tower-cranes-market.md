<p><p>The high-rise building tower cranes market includes various types designed for specific construction needs. Self-erecting tower cranes are compact and can be easily set up, suitable for smaller projects. Flat top tower cranes feature a flat working platform, offering enhanced safety and visibility. Hammerhead tower cranes, known for their horizontal jib, provide substantial lifting capacity and radius. Luffing jib tower cranes are ideal for tight spaces, allowing the jib to raise and lower without moving the entire crane, making them versatile for complex sites.</p></p>
