<p><ul><li>Insertion</li><li>Portable</li><li>Inline</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal energy flow metering solutions are categorized into three main types: insertion, portable, and inline. Insertion meters are designed to be inserted into existing pipes, providing a cost-effective option for retrofitting. Portable meters are versatile instruments used for temporary measurements, ideal for troubleshooting and testing in various locations. Inline meters are permanently installed within the flow system, offering continuous monitoring and higher accuracy for process control. Each type serves specific applications based on measurement needs and operational requirements.</p></p>
