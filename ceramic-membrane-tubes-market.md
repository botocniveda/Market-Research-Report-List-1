<p><p>The ceramic membrane tubes market is primarily segmented into two types: alumina and silicon carbide (SiC). Alumina membranes are known for their excellent thermal stability and chemical resistance, making them ideal for applications in wastewater treatment and food processing. In contrast, SiC membranes offer enhanced permeability and mechanical strength, suitable for harsher environments, including petrochemical and pharmaceutical processes. Both types cater to distinct industrial needs, driven by their unique properties and performance in various filtration applications.</p></p>
