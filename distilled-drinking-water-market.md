<p><p>The distilled drinking water market can be categorized into single and double distillation types. Single distillation involves heating water to create steam, which is then condensed back into liquid, effectively removing impurities and contaminants. This method is efficient for general purification needs. In contrast, double distillation undergoes the process twice, enhancing purity by eliminating even trace impurities, and offering higher quality water. These variations cater to different consumer preferences and requirements for water purity in various applications.</p></p>
