<p><p>Underwater pelletizing systems utilize various types of extruders to convert thermoplastic materials into pellets. The single screw extruder pelletizing line employs a single screw for melting and conveying materials, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, the double screw extruder pelletizing line features two intermeshing screws, providing enhanced mixing and homogeneity for more complex materials. Other market types may include specialized systems designed for unique applications, offering customization to meet specific production requirements in the plastic processing industry.</p></p>
