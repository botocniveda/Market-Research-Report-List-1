<p><p>The SMS Firewall market primarily consists of two types of messaging: Application to Person (A2P) messaging and Person to Application (P2A) messaging. A2P messaging involves businesses sending text messages to consumers for notifications, alerts, or promotions, enhancing customer engagement. Conversely, P2A messaging allows consumers to send messages to applications, such as chatbots or customer service systems, facilitating two-way communication. Both segments play crucial roles in ensuring secure and efficient messaging while preventing spam and ensuring message delivery.</p></p>
