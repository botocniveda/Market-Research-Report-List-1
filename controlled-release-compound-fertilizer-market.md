<p><p>The controlled-release compound fertilizer market is divided into two main types: polymer-coated and non-polymer coated fertilizers. Polymer-coated fertilizers consist of granules encased in a polymer layer, gradually releasing nutrients over time, which promotes efficient nutrient uptake and reduces leaching. In contrast, non-polymer coated fertilizers rely on other mechanisms, such as chemical formulations or physical mixes, to control nutrient release rates. Both types cater to specific agricultural needs, enhancing crop yield while optimizing nutrient management.</p></p>
