<p><p>The City Surveillance Camera market comprises two main types: centralized IP cameras and decentralized IP cameras. Centralized IP cameras transmit data to a central server for processing and storage, offering comprehensive control and easy integration into existing systems. In contrast, decentralized IP cameras operate independently, processing and storing data locally or in the cloud, which enhances scalability and reduces bandwidth use. Both types are essential for urban security, providing flexibility and efficiency in monitoring public spaces.</p></p>
