<p><p>Membrane separation systems encompass various technologies used for filtration and purification processes. Microfiltration removes larger particles and microorganisms, while ultrafiltration targets smaller macromolecules. Nanofiltration offers selective removal of divalent ions and small organic molecules. Reverse osmosis provides high-efficiency desalination and purification by filtering out nearly all dissolved substances. Chromatography is essential for separating compounds based on their chemical properties, while ion exchange focuses on the exchange of ions between a solution and a solid medium, enhancing water treatment and purification.</p></p>
