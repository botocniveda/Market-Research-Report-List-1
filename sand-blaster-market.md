<p><p>The sand blaster market is primarily divided into two types: dry blasters and wet blasters. Dry blasters use compressed air to propel abrasive materials onto surfaces, making them ideal for tasks like rust removal and surface preparation. In contrast, wet blasters combine water with the abrasive medium, reducing airborne dust and minimizing surface heat, making them suitable for delicate materials and environmentally sensitive applications. Both technologies cater to various industries, including automotive, construction, and manufacturing, addressing specific cleaning and finishing needs.</p></p>
