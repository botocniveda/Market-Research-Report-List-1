<p><ul><li>Traditional Sandbag</li><li>Sandless Sandbag</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flood bag market consists of traditional sandbags and sandless sandbags. Traditional sandbags are filled with sand, offering sturdy, biodegradable barriers for flood control but are labor-intensive and challenging to transport. In contrast, sandless sandbags utilize lightweight materials, such as polymers or absorbent compounds, that inflate upon contact with water, facilitating easier deployment and storage. These modern alternatives provide effective flood protection while reducing logistical burdens, making them increasingly popular in disaster management and flood prevention strategies.</p></p>
