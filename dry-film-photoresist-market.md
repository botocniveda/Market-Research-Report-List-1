<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Dry Film Photoresist Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive</li><li>Negative</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dry Film Photoresist market is categorized into two main types: positive and negative resists. Positive photoresists become soluble in the developer solution when exposed to light, allowing the exposed areas to be removed, which is useful for creating detailed patterns. Conversely, negative photoresists harden when exposed to light, making the unexposed areas removable. These two types are essential for various applications in electronics, printed circuit boards, and microelectronics, catering to diverse production needs and design requirements.</p></p>
