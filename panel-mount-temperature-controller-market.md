<p><p>The panel-mount temperature controller market includes three main types: switch controllers, proportional controllers, and PID controllers. Switch controllers operate by turning heating or cooling devices on or off at set temperatures. Proportional controllers provide a variable output based on the temperature difference, allowing for more precise control. PID controllers combine proportional, integral, and derivative actions to minimize error, ensuring stable temperature conditions. Each type caters to varying control needs, efficiency, and application requirements in industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
