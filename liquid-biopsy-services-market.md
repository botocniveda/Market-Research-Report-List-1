<p><strong>The Liquid Biopsy Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CTC Detection</li><li>ctDNA Detection</li><li>Exosome Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid biopsy services market encompasses three primary types: Circulating Tumor Cell (CTC) detection, circulating tumor DNA (ctDNA) detection, and exosome detection. CTC detection focuses on isolating and analyzing tumor cells from blood samples, providing insights into cancer presence and progression. ctDNA detection involves identifying fragments of tumor DNA in the bloodstream, offering crucial information about genetic mutations and treatment response. Exosome detection analyzes microvesicles released by cells, facilitating the study of tumor signaling and cell communication, enhancing cancer diagnostics and monitoring.</p></p>
