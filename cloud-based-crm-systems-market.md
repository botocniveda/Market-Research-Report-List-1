<p><p>Cloud-based CRM systems can be categorized into two main market types: SaaS (Software as a Service) and PaaS (Platform as a Service). SaaS solutions, typically found in the public cloud, offer accessible, subscription-based CRM software that requires minimal infrastructure management. In contrast, PaaS solutions are often private cloud-based, providing a customizable platform where businesses can develop and modify CRM applications tailored to their needs, while ensuring higher control over data security and compliance. Both models enhance flexibility and scalability for organizations.</p></p>
