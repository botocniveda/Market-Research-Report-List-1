<p><p>The cylinder vacuum cleaner market is primarily divided into two types: bagged and bagless. Bagged cylinder vacuum cleaners use disposable bags to collect dirt and debris, offering efficient filtration and easy disposal but requiring regular bag replacements. Conversely, bagless models utilize a dust container that can be emptied and reused, providing convenience and eliminating the need for bags. However, they may necessitate more frequent filter cleaning. Both types serve diverse consumer preferences, balancing efficiency, maintenance, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
