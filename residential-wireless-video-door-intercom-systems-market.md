<p><p>Residential wireless video door intercom systems are categorized mainly into Wi-Fi and Radio Frequency (RF) types. Wi-Fi systems utilize existing home internet networks, allowing high-quality video and audio transmission with extensive range, ideal for smart home integration. In contrast, RF systems operate on specific radio frequencies, offering reliable communication without relying on internet connectivity. RF systems are often easier to install and may work in areas with limited Wi-Fi access, making them suitable for a broader range of residential environments.</p></p>
