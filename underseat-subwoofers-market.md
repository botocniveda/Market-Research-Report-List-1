<p><p>The underseat subwoofers market is categorized into two main types: active and passive subwoofers. Active subwoofers integrate an amplifier within the unit, providing a compact solution that simplifies installation and optimizes performance. Conversely, passive subwoofers require an external amplifier for power, offering more flexibility in sound customization but needing additional components for setup. Both types cater to varying consumer preferences in audio quality and installation convenience, enhancing the listening experience in vehicles and small spaces.</p></p>
