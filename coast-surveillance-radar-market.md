<p><p>Coast surveillance radar systems are primarily classified into X-Band and S-Band radar types, along with other specialized radar systems. X-Band radars operate at higher frequencies, providing higher resolution imaging and enhanced detection capabilities, ideal for monitoring small targets in coastal areas. In contrast, S-Band radars operate at lower frequencies, offering longer-range detection and better performance in adverse weather conditions. Other radar types may include multi-band or frequency-modulated continuous wave systems, catering to specific surveillance needs and applications in maritime environments.</p></p>
