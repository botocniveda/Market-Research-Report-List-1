<p><p>The Scraping Grader market includes two primary types: Twin Shaft Type and Triple Shaft Type. The Twin Shaft Type features two parallel shafts, enhancing efficiency by allowing for continuous material mixing and grading, ideal for medium to large-scale operations. In contrast, the Triple Shaft Type incorporates three shafts, providing even greater mixing capability and precision in grading, making it suitable for complex materials and applications requiring higher throughput. Both types are essential in industries like construction and mining for material processing.</p></p>
