<p><p>Composite pipes for water treatment are categorized primarily into clad pipes and non-metal composite pipes. Clad pipes feature a combination of materials, typically a metal core covered by a corrosion-resistant layer, providing enhanced durability and protection against harsh conditions. Conversely, non-metal composite pipes are made from materials such as fiberglass or polymer composites, offering lightweight, flexibility, and resistance to chemicals and pressure. Both types are designed to improve performance and longevity in water treatment applications, ensuring efficient fluid transport.</p></p>
