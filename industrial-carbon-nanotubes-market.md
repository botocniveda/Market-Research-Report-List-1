<p><p>The industrial carbon nanotubes market comprises two primary types: Single Wall Carbon Nanotubes (SWCNTs) and Multi-Walled Carbon Nanotubes (MWCNTs). SWCNTs consist of a single layer of graphene rolled into a cylindrical shape, offering unique electrical and thermal properties suited for applications in electronics and nanocomposites. In contrast, MWCNTs consist of multiple concentric layers of graphene, providing enhanced mechanical strength and conductivity, making them ideal for various applications in materials science, energy storage, and reinforced composites.</p></p>
