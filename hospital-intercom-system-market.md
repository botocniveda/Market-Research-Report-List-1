<p><p>The Hospital Intercom System market is divided into two main types: wired and wireless systems. Wired systems utilize physical cabling for communication, offering reliable connectivity and minimal interference, making them ideal for larger facilities. Conversely, wireless systems employ radio frequencies for communication, providing flexibility and ease of installation, especially in retrofitting spaces. Both systems enhance communication among medical staff, ensuring quick response times and improved patient care. The choice between them often depends on hospital needs and infrastructure.</p></p>
