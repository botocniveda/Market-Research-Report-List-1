<p><p>Search Engine Marketing (SEM) solutions encompass various pricing models to fit advertisers' needs. Cost Per Time (CPT) charges based on the duration an ad is displayed. Cost-Per-Acquisition (CPA) focuses on the cost incurred for each successful conversion. Cost-Per-Click (CPC) requires payment for each click on the ad, while Cost-Per-Mille (CPM) entails costs for every thousand impressions. Additionally, there are other models like Cost-Per-Lead (CPL) or hybrid approaches, offering flexibility for campaign strategies and budget management.</p></p>
